About

WHO WE ARE

Established in 2018, Bliss Pontsho Trading and Projects Pty Ltd is new to the construction industry with a hunger and passion to provide safe and reliable civil infrastructure and building structures to our rainbow nation and beyond.The director, Bliss Maimela Modiba, a young black South African male aspiring to be a light of inspiration to others wishing to join the construction industry. BP Trading and Projects believes in providing safe and reliable buildings that will be able to withstand unfavorable weather conditions or natural disasters.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

We specialize in providing only safe and reliable civil infrastructure and building structures using the finest materials for strong healthcare facilities, schools and your general buildings such as shops and houses. Hiring only competent and key personnel, we serve all our clients with excellence, dignity and integrity using the latest technology.

WHAT WE VALUE

• Innovative and critical thinking

• Trust and Transparency

• Integrity and Honour

• Politeness and Respect
